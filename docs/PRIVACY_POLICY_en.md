# Startpage Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

This policy explains the types of data Startpage may process while providing its features.

Startpage may process account data such as email, password hashes, verification-code state, sessions, and invite-code relationships; page data such as quick links, notes, subscriptions, hot-list preferences, theme settings, and search preferences; operational data such as request time, error state, and necessary hosting logs; and third-party data needed for weather, hot lists, wallpapers, and email verification.

The data is used to support login, registration, preference sync, home-screen content, verification emails, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting. Startpage is not designed to sell user data.

Some settings are stored locally in the browser. Account data, invite codes, and data that needs cross-device access may be processed through cloud services such as Cloudflare Worker and D1. The exact scope may change as features evolve.

Do not store passwords, keys, payment details, or other sensitive information in notes, quick links, or subscription settings. Clearing browser data may remove locally stored settings.

Startpage may use services such as Resend, Cloudflare, Vercel, weather APIs, hot-list APIs, and wallpaper APIs. Third-party services process requests according to their own policies.
